Karpom Karpipom

This project aims to identify committed students who, despite originating from underprivileged backgrounds and not pursuing higher education, harbor ambitious academic goals. The initiative is designed to assist them in excelling in their board exams. Over the years, during the admission process of Maatram Foundation, it became evident that many young students encountered difficulties in achieving high marks due to a lack of qualified teachers, insufficient infrastructure, and, in numerous cases, challenging domestic circumstances. Consequently, our goal is to elevate their education by engaging former students as mentors and educators through alumni volunteers, thus bridging the existing gap. This initiative will not only help them excel in their board exams but also provide them with an outstanding opportunity to pursue higher education through Maatram Foundation. The entire initiative is managed and overseen by the Maatram Alumni Association.
