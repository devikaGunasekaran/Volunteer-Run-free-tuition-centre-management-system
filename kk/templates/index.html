About Us

Karpom Karpipom is a Maatram foundation project, identifying aspiring students from economically deprived backgrounds and providing tutoring to help them excel in their Board exams. The initiative addresses challenges like lack of qualified teachers, inadequate infrastructure, and difficult domestic conditions. Managed by the Maatram Alumni Association, alumni volunteers and student volunteers offer mentorship and academic support.
